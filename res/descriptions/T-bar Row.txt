(2x10, 2x8 @ RPE 9)

SUPERSET: Pull-ups (AMRAP @ RPE 8, use jumping negatives/bands need be)

Hang from a bar with a pronated (palms facing away) grip. Place your hands about shoulder-width apart. Pull up and raise your body until your chin reaches the same height as the bar. Avoid swinging or kipping as you pull.
