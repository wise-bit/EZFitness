Start with a dumbbell behind your head, keeping your elbow 45 degrees from your head. Place your free hand on the bicep of the working arm, stabilizing it. Lockout your working arm, and end with it behind your head to complete muffin. 
3 sets, 12 - 10 - 8 Reps


-- INTERMEDIATE MODE --

Attach a rope to the bottom pulley of the pulley machine. Grasping the rope with both hands, extend your arms with your hands directly above your head. Your elbows should be in close to your head and the arms should be perpendicular to the floor with the knuckles aimed at the ceiling.Slowly lower the rope behind your head as you hold the upper arms stationary.
3 sets, 10 - 8 - 10 Reps   


-- HARDMODE --

(4x12 @ RPE 10)

Attach a rope to the bottom pulley of the pulley machine. Hold the rope with both hands, extend your arms with your hands directly above your heads using a neutral grip(palms facing each other). Elbows should close to your head and arms should be perpendicular to the floor with the knuckles aimed at the ceiling. This will be your starting position. Slowly lower the rope behind your head. Then slowly return to the starting position.
