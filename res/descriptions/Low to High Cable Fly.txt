-- INTERMEDIATE MODE --

Start with both ends of a cable machine at the bottom position. Grab the handles and without bending too much at the elbow, bring your hands together out in front of your chest. This creates a down and out to a up and in range of motion.
3 sets, 10 - 8 - 10 Reps

(4x12 @ RPE 10)

SUPERSET: High to Low Cable Fly (same reps @ RPE 10)

A Cable fly or Cable flye is a strength training exercise in which the hand and arm move through an arc while the elbow is kept at a constant angle. Flies are used to work the muscles of the upper body.
