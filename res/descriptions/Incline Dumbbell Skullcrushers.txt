-- INTERMEDIATE MODE --

Lie back on an incline bench with a dumbbell in each hand. Hold the weights over your head, palms facing each other. Bend your elbows and lower the weights to the sides of your head.
3 sets, 10 - 8 - 10 Reps   


-- HARDMODE --

(3x12 @ RPE 9)

SUPERSET: Incline Tricep Kickbacks (same reps @ RPE 9)

Your upper arms should be close to your torso and parallel to the floor. There should be a 90 - degree angle formed between your forearm and upper arm. This is your starting position. Now, while keeping your uppers arms stationary, exhale and use your triceps to lift the weights until the arm is fully extended.
